gReader 1.1b

If you see this screen you've successfully installed and started gReader 1.1b

After entering your google reader username and password, the app needs to:

  1. Start the nook's WIFI
  2. Login to Google
  3. Access your Google Reader items
  4. Download the images for each item
When all of this is complete, the app will show you your first item in your reading list.

To advance to the next item, the default button is Left Right (left side Right). The previous item can be be found by pressing Left Left. To scroll an item down use Right Right, and to scroll back up use Right Left.
